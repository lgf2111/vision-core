How It Works

1. Request for repair

Sign up for a free account on our website. You can either pay the flat price as stated, or request for a quote with our free diagnostics. All payments will be done online on our website. Either drop off your item in person, or use our two-way delivery service, and await the status of your item.

2. Await for item status

Check your account on this website for constant updates on your item. You can also choose your preferred mode of communication. If needed, you can chat with our chatbot or call our phone number (+65 9812 3456).

3. Collect repaired item

After your item has been repaired, you can collect it in person or via delivery to your house.
